Pricing FAQ

Answers to common questions about retainers, setup fees, and contract terms

Are there any setup fees?

No, we do not charge setup fees. We use the first 14 days of our engagement to complete account audits and strategy mapping under the standard monthly rate.

What is the minimum contract term?

We operate on rolling 30-day agreements. You can cancel or pause your retainer at any point with a 15-day notice period before the next billing cycle.

Do your retainers include ad spend?

No, ad spend is billed directly to your credit card by Google or Meta. Our fees cover the management, asset creation, optimization, and attribution tracking of campaigns.
